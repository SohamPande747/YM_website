Plastic आणि बरंच काही

To spread awareness about the threat of using single-use plastic Yuva Marathi had organised an essay competition among students to emphasize the need for elimination of Single-Use Plastic and allied topics.

Gudhi Padwa

Gudhipadwa, the grandest of our events, is the celebration of the Marathi New Year with pomp and fervour. In the morning, eminent guests and speakers are invited to perform the ‘Gudhi Pujan’ followed by a performance of Dhol-tasha and Dhwaj. Later, in the evening, the members of our dance, drama and music academies showcase their immense talent and put on a fantastic show that is attended by VIT’s Hon. Chancellor and many famous celebrities! But the real highlight of the event is in fact the huge dinner spread of delicious Maharashtrian food, which is eagerly waited for by all.
